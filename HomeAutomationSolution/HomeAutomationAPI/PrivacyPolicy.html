General

This skill is meant for students and parents of kids studying at Chirec International School, India. Since school lunch is an important part of the facilties offered by the school and kids & parents are equally interested in knowing the school lunch menu for a week, this skill provides a unique and simple way to get that information by asking Alexa. The skill relies on the weekly lunch menu published by the school and simply translates that information into spoken English for everyone.

Targeted Audience

Students and Parents

Information Collected

No information is collected by this skill as it simply provides a read-only mechanism for the audience to know about an already published piece of information
